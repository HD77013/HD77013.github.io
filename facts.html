Here are some fun facts

  • Breaking Bad won 16 Primetime Emmy awards
  • It's show was the first and only to reach a rating of 10/10 for one of it's episodes on IMDB
  • It takes $3 million to produce one episode
  • The meth produced for the show is made from rock candy
  • The actor for Jesse Pinkman, Aaron Paul, never took any acting lessons
  • Some aspects of the show were heavily inspired by the movie Scarface
